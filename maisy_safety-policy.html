Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Chatting Maisy is committed to fostering a safe and respectful digital environment. We strictly prohibit all forms of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. Our commitment includes:

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Any content that depicts, promotes, or supports CSAE is strictly forbidden and will result in immediate action, including removal and reporting to the appropriate authorities.

2. Proactive Monitoring

We actively monitor for harmful content using both automated tools and human reviewers to identify and address any CSAE-related materials.

3. User Education and Reporting

We provide resources to educate users on recognizing and reporting CSAE content. A clear and accessible reporting mechanism is available within our app for users to flag such content.

4. Collaboration with Authorities

Chatting Maisy cooperates fully with law enforcement and child protection organizations to ensure timely intervention in addressing any CSAE violations.

5. Compliance with Global Standards

We align our policies and procedures with globally recognized frameworks, including the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child, to uphold the safety and dignity of children.

Together, we aim to protect vulnerable individuals and create a secure and supportive community for all users.
